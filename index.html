Biography

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known simply as Vijay, is a prominent Indian actor and playback singer in the Tamil film industry. Born on June 22, 1974, he made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Vetri" (1984). Over the years, Vijay has starred in numerous blockbusters and earned a massive fan following for his charisma and versatile performances.

He is also known for his philanthropic activities and social initiatives.

Filmography

  • Bigil (2019) - A sports drama where he plays a football coach.
  • Master (2021) - A film that became a huge success during the pandemic.
